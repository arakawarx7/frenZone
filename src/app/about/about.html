Aaron Arakawa

Born and raised from Aiea Hawaii, Aaron Arakawa has always had a passion for computers. It wasnt until his mid 20s where he started to pursue his passion. Aaron has lived in Hawaii for most of his life. In his early 20s He resided in a few spots in Southern California, Huntington Beach, West Hollywood, and Anaheim then moved back to Hawaii where he soon started Devleague to be come a full stack developer. Aaron loves traveling, whiskey, golf, hanging out with his friends, longs walks on the beach, and watching the sunset.
